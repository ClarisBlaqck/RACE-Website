Strategic Research
for Sustainable Development

RACE is the link between research and community development. "Research Access and Community Empowerment" reflects the core activities of the NGO, which involve collecting research findings from university libraries and implementing them in local communities in Cameroon. The word "Access" signifies the goal of making research findings accessible to the community, while "Community Empowerment" highlights the aim of empowering local communities through the utilization of research. The acronym "RACE" is a concise and memorable representation of the organization's mission and activities.
